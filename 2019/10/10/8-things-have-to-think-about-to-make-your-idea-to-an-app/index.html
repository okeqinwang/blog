8 Things have to Think about to Make Your Idea To an App

Photo by Chris Adamus on [Unsplash]

0x1 The story begins with an apple

Like an apple falling off a tree and hitting you on the head, suddenly you have an idea about how to make the product land on quickly. The middle way is beyond our imagination. If you only have good product thinking, but lack code implementation ability, you need to find a technician to assist you. Or if you are already comprehensive, you can skip the prototype directly, deduce it in your mind, and then start to roll it up. In fact, I strongly recommend that prototypes be displayed in front of us. After all, they are completely imagined in the brain, and it’s hard to think about them thoroughly. Moreover, The product prototype design is only the first step to start a pit.It’s a great honor for me to meet you here and share something that you probably know or don’t know, and that I know exactly.

0x2 Prototype Design Tool

  • Flinto
  • Axure

0x3 Front-end technology selection（Hybrid App）

  • Vue
  • Uinapp
  • Weex
  • ReactNative
  • Element-UI | Ant-Design

0x4 Backstage Science and Technology Selection

  • Basic framework: Java-SpringCloud | SpringBoot | Dubbo
  • Protocol selection : HTTP vs TCP
  • Selection of databases : SQL vs NOSQL (Mysql | Redis)
  • Auth Tickets: Session | OAuth | JWT
  • Message Queue: RocketMq
  • GateWay: Nginx | Openresty

0x5 Deployment Automation

  • Jenkins | Ansible
  • Docker
  • Kubernetes

0x6 Code Hosting

  • Github
  • Gitlab | Gogs

0x7 Monitoring & Visualization

  • Cat
  • Prometheus
  • Pinpoint
  • E（Elasticsearch）L（Logstash）K（Kibana）, ELK
  • Grafana | Kibana can be combined with Proetheus to form monitoring data integration

0x8 Others . (All stack developers will know)

  • JavaScript
  • Vuejs
  • Python
  • Postman
  • Whistle
  • WireShark
  • Tcpdump
